Words I carry
for you every day

From the moment you entered my life, something shifted — the quiet became warmer, the days became lighter, and without even meaning to, you made the world make sense to me. You were a stranger once. I can barely remember that version of the world.

Watching you grow into the woman you are becoming has been one of the great privileges of my life. You carry strength in the way you speak, grace in the way you move, and a quiet fire that I don't think you fully see in yourself yet. But I see it. I always have.

"She is clothed with strength and dignity,
and she laughs without fear of the future."
— Proverbs 31:25

In your strength, I see the reflection of divine purpose — a woman forged in quiet courage, walking through each day with the grace of one who knows her worth is written in eternal ink. Your dignity isn't just in how you carry yourself, but in how you lift others, how you speak truth with love, how you stand firm in the face of storms that would scatter lesser hearts.

I love what God is doing in and through you, Standwa Sam. This chapter — your twenties — is going to be extraordinary. Not because everything will be perfect, but because of who you are walking into it as.

Today I celebrate you. Not just the birthday, but the entire glorious fact of you — every laugh, every quiet moment, every dream you've ever let me witness. You deserve all of it. And infinitely more.

"The Lord will guide you always;
he will satisfy your needs in a sun-scorched land."
— Isaiah 58:11

As you step into this new season, may you feel His guidance like a gentle breeze on a summer morning — constant, sure, leading you through deserts and valleys alike. In the dry places of life, when the world feels barren and hope seems distant, remember that He who called you is faithful. He will provide streams in the wilderness, refreshment for your soul, and strength for the journey ahead.

May this year pull you closer to everything you are meant to be. I cannot wait to witness every single chapter.

"Charm is deceptive, and beauty is fleeting;
but a woman who fears the Lord is to be praised."
— Proverbs 31:30

Your beauty radiates from within — a luminous quality that time cannot diminish, that circumstances cannot tarnish. It is the beauty of a heart fully surrendered, of eyes that see the world through lenses of grace, of hands that serve with quiet devotion. This is the beauty that lasts, the beauty that draws others to the light within you, the beauty that reflects the very heart of our Creator.

"May He grant you according to your heart's desire, And fulfill all your purposes."
— Psalm 20:4

May your deepest desires align with His perfect will, and may you find in Him the fulfillment of every noble aspiration. As you dream, as you hope, as you step forward in faith, know that the One who knit you together has plans beyond your wildest imaginings — plans for good, for growth, for glory that will unfold like a masterpiece across the canvas of your life.

Forever yours — aujourd'hui, demain, et pour toujours. ❤️
